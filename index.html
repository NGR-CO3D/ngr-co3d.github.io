Neural Geometry and Rendering: Advances and the Common Objects in 3D Challenge @ECCV2022

The success of neural geometry and rendering has created enormous interest in 3D reconstruction and its potential in graphics and image understanding. The goal of this full-day workshop is to bring together the neural geometry and graphics communities to discuss recent advances and future challenges, such as reconstructing dynamic events from limited views. We will have a number of distinguished speakers and a new competition, based on a novel edition of the Common Objects in 3D (CO3D) benchmark, an honorable mention from last year's ICCV.

More details will be announced shortly.
